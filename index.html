Love Letter to Life

There are many inspirations we should have in life.

My first is for a woman called Li Fang, she is one of those women with fire in her heart and unbounded love for the world; she is one who always wants to clean up her own town; to give herself to those who couldn't look for themselves; who wanted to clean up for the community she was part of; She simply hoped that the people around her were better off from her efforts. In doing so she has transformed many lives, including my own.

When it is time for her to go, I know that my tears will not be for her, but for the causes she cared for; there will be those who never will have known her love; lives saved by her affection;

Let us all find our opportunity in life to give thanks for all those who have aimed to help us before, and hope that we can give as much life and love to others.

I hope that my work can impact as many as she has in her life time.
